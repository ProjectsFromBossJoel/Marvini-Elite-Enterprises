About Us

We exist to fix Ghana's food trust gap.

M Digital Food Chain is a proudly Ghanaian company built on a simple belief: everyone deserves safe, high-quality food — whether it's a hot meal delivered to your door or a jar of natural stew on your shelf.

We are a subsidiary of Marvini Elite Enterprises, and our mission is to modernise the way Ghanaians eat, cook, and trust their food.

FAQ

Common questions.

How do you certify a restaurant?+
We conduct physical kitchen inspections, check health certificates for all food handlers, and ensure compliance with FDA standards — repeated regularly.
Are your jarred foods really preservative-free?+
Yes. We use pressure canning — a century-old natural method — to preserve food without adding any chemicals. Just pure ingredients and natural flavours.
Where do you get your ingredients?+
Directly from local farms and fisheries across Ghana. We prioritise fresh, seasonal produce from our partner farmers.
Is delivery temperature controlled?+
Absolutely. We use insulated packaging and temperature-managed logistics so your meal arrives hot and fresh.
How do I become a vendor?+
Visit our "For Vendors" section above or contact us via email — we'll guide you through certification step by step.
